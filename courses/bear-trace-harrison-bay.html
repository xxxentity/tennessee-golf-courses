About Bear Trace at Harrison Bay

Bear Trace at Harrison Bay is a stunning Jack Nicklaus Signature Design golf course nestled along the shores of Chickamauga Lake in Harrison, Tennessee. This championship 18-hole course offers golfers an unforgettable experience with its dramatic elevation changes, strategic water features, and breathtaking views of the Tennessee Valley.

Opened in 2002, Bear Trace represents the Golden Bear's commitment to creating challenging yet fair golf courses that work in harmony with the natural landscape. The course features bentgrass greens, Bermuda fairways, and plays to 7,012 yards from the championship tees.

What sets Bear Trace apart is its spectacular lakefront setting. Several holes offer stunning views of Chickamauga Lake, while the course's elevation changes provide panoramic vistas of the surrounding Tennessee mountains. The signature hole, a par-3 over water, is consistently ranked among the most beautiful holes in the Southeast.
