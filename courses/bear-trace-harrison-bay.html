About Bear Trace at Harrison Bay

Bear Trace at Harrison Bay is a stunning Jack Nicklaus Signature Design golf course that offers an exceptional golfing experience along the scenic shores of Chickamauga Lake. This championship 18-hole course combines challenging play with breathtaking natural beauty, making it a must-play destination for golfers visiting Tennessee.

The course features dramatic elevation changes, strategic water hazards, and meticulously maintained fairways and greens. With its resort-quality amenities and public accessibility, Bear Trace provides an upscale golf experience that rivals private clubs in the region.
